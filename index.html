GreenLog

Your eco-friendly habit tracker

Welcome to GreenLog, the app that helps you track and celebrate your environmentally friendly actions. Log daily activities like recycling, biking to work, and conserving water. Set personalized goals, view your progress over time, and see the positive impact you're making on the planet.

Log an Action

Quickly record any eco-action you take—whether it's using a reusable bag, reducing water usage, or supporting local produce. Customize action categories and add notes to reflect your experience.

Set a Goal

Define weekly or monthly sustainability goals. Whether it's reducing waste by 20% or planting trees, GreenLog helps you stay motivated with reminders and milestones.

View Your Progress

Explore charts and summaries of your logged actions. Track trends, identify areas for improvement, and share your achievements with friends and community.
